Welcome to Team Initiative

Team Initiative Limited

TIL is a professional organization designed to provide consultancy services in areas of management; social work; monitoring and evaluation, reproductive health, Livelihoods, HIV/AIDS, Adolescents, Youths and Orphans and Vulnerable Children (OVCs) gender; project implementation as well as institutional and technical capacity strengthening.

Our goal

Our goal is to contribute to the improvement of quality services delivery in societies served by our clientele.

OUR CLIENTS

Team Initiative Limited
Best Provider for Human Resource and Technical Capacity

We have a pool of specialists with diverse academic and work backgrounds and experience. This has enabled us to successfully execute high profile assignments in our carefully chosen domains. Our professionals are specialized in the fields of; Social work, Agriculture, Nutrition, public and community health, monitoring and evaluation, research, anthropology, epidemiology, environmental health, Institutional Development, Business management, Economics, sociology and policy and program governance. In addition, our professionals have extensive knowledge and skills in training in different social and community development endeavours as well as research and development. We also have a database for short-term consultants serving as; Task Specific Specialists, Field Supervisors, Field data collectors, and Data entry Clerks.
